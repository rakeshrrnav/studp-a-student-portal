Information about KREIS

The candidates who qualify the KRIES Admission Test 2020, will get the admission into the Karnataka Residential Educational Institutions. So, the candidates must submit the KRIES 6th Class Online Application Form as soon as possible the link will be active. Students should submit the application forms through online mode only. While the Morarji Desai Admission Test for the 6th Class helps the candidates to get admission into the schools of KREIS. Every year a huge number of candidates have appeared the KREIS Entrance Exam 2020 and getting the admission into the schools. The selection of the candidates will be based on the candidate’s performance in the written test. For more information about the KREIS 6th Class Notification 2020 visit our web portal regularly.

Educational Qualification: Candidates must complete the 5th Class to apply for the KREIS Entrance Exam 2020

    Morarji Desai Residential Schools

    Morarji Desai Residential Schools for Minorities are the schools in Karnataka state in India. These are run by Directorate of Minorities department of the Government of Karnataka. The schools are set up to provide free residential education from Standards VI to X to the students from minority communities

    Kittur Rani Chennamma Residential School (only Girls)

    Kittur Rani Chennamma Residential Schools are exclusively meant for girls students. Equality is maintained without discrimination in curricular and extra-curricular activities and also in boarding and hostel facilities. The students belongs to various castes and communities are studying under a single roof without any prejudices in sex, religion, and caste.
